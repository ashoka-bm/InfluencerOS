# InfluencerOS Voice Profile

Use this when writing about InfluencerOS itself.

## Style

- Direct.
- Concrete.
- Practical.
- Systems-aware.
- Clear about boundaries and tradeoffs.

## Avoid

- Hype.
- Claims that imply automated publishing or generation in v1.
- Vague terms such as "magic memory" or "autonomous content machine."
- Platform-specific claims unless a workflow explicitly supports them.
