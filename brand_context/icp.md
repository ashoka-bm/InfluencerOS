# InfluencerOS ICP

Early ICP: operators building or managing avatar-led creators who need repeatable research, planning, provenance, and generation-readiness before media creation.

## Needs

- Keep creator identity and memory separated.
- Turn current research into creator-fit ideas.
- Produce provider-neutral generation plans.
- Trace outputs back to evidence and decisions.
- Improve over time without blending creator learnings.
