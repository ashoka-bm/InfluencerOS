# Initial Campaign And Pressure Scope Stays Small

The first implementation adds the Campaign hierarchy, Content Opportunity path, typed commercial expression, deterministic Commercial Pressure, basic calendar-horizon Pressure projection, direct-reference validation, and rebuildable Campaign and Concept evaluation over existing Project status, publication, analytics, and Performance Summary records. It stores enough stable source facts for future analytics without building speculative learning machinery.

Deferred work includes Campaign Revisions and Waves, Pressure Experiments, cross-creator benchmarks, evidence-maturity automation, automatic policy recommendations, advanced attribution, assisted-Campaign allocation, mandatory multi-window analytics, and automatic audience-harm stopping. Deferred terms remain documented design targets, not schema, workflow, or validation obligations in this slice.
